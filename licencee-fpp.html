Flight crew licence application

Back

CASA must ensure flight crew licence applicants are fit and proper before issuing a licence. If you answer yes to any of the following questions you must provide further details and your application will need to be further assessed by CASA after submission.

Has any action been taken against you; or is any action in the process of being taken against you; or have you been refused the issue of any aviation related licence, certificate, rating or authority by any organisation?

Have you ever been refused the issue of a transport related licence or certificate? (eg, pilot’s licence, pilot certificate, driver’s licence, boating licence)

Do you have any criminal conviction or finding of guilt, which is less than ten years old, or any juvenile criminal conviction or finding of guilt, which is less than five years old? Note: You should also include all motor vehicle traffic-related convictions including those from overseas.

Is suspension or cancellation action pending in relation to any aviation licence you hold?
