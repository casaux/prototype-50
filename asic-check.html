ASIC card verification

CASA requires the applicant has undergone a security check when issuing a flight crew licence. Aviation security identification card (ASIC) holders can verify their card below.

If you do not yet have an ASIC, or you have an AVID, you cannot finish this application online and will need to apply for your licence using the relevent form.

Your issuing body is who you applied to and received your ASIC from. If you're unsure of this, please refer to any correspondence you received regarding your ASIC application or contact your flight school.
Please enter the number shown on your ASIC. This is usually at the bottom of the ASIC and may contain letters and dashes. Please enter all the characters as shown on your ASIC.
Please enter your first name as shown on card. This will be checked along with your last name WODEN and your date of birth (01 04 1980).
