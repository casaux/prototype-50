Lodgement of photograph

CASA requires a photograph of the applicant when issuing a flight crew licence.

As a valid aviation security identification card (ASIC) holder, CASA is able to use the photograph supplied for your ASIC for this purpose with your consent.

If you do not consent to CASA using this photograph, you cannot finish this application online and will need to apply for your licence using the relevent form.
